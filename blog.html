a new start - 09.16.2024

Today marks the beginning of something new. After months of planning, overthinking, and second-guessing, I've finally decided to embrace the uncertainty that comes with starting fresh.

The tech industry moves fast—faster than most of us can keep up with. Just when you think you've mastered a framework, three new ones have already emerged. It's exhausting, but also exhilarating. There's something beautiful about being in a constant state of learning, where every day presents an opportunity to be humbled by how much you don't know.

I've been at Verizon for a while now, working on AI solutions and ML models. The work is challenging in ways I never anticipated. It's not just about writing clean code or implementing algorithms—it's about understanding people, their needs, and how technology can serve them without losing the human element.

My fiancé and I have been planning our next adventure. We've been talking about Japan for years, and something about this season of life feels right for taking that leap. There's something about immersing yourself in a completely different culture that forces you to grow in ways you can't predict.

Photography has been my escape lately. There's a meditative quality to capturing moments—trying to freeze time in a way that tells a story without words. I've been experimenting with film again, embracing the imperfections that come with analog processes. Sometimes the most beautiful things happen when you relinquish control.

This blog is part of that new beginning. A space to document the journey, the failures, the small victories, and everything in between. Not for anyone else necessarily, but as a reminder to myself that growth happens in the messy middle, not at the destination.
